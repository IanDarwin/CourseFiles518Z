Levin on Android

Jonathan Levin has a series of books on the internals of the Android System. Volume 1 is "Power User's" detailed look at the system as a whole. Volume 2 is the actual internals, covering many topics that don't fit into Vol 1. Volume 3 isn't out yet.

Volume 1 was originally sold in paper and in eBook form. Unfortunately for Levin, a copy of the eBook was included in a large tranche of material that WikiLeaks "obtained" from a careless CIA agent, and the book was thus available for free across the Internet, resulting in the author withdrawing from selling it. The copy reproduced here is included with the author's permission, in hopes that a few people who are really interested in the topic will purchase the updated Volume 1 and the new (2022) Volume 2 which goes into much more detail.
